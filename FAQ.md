# FAQ

---

**Q: Is the app free to use?**  
A: The app is free if you use open-source LLMs (like Llama or Mixtral). Using Azure LLMs requires payment.

---

**Q: Why do we need Redis?**  
A: Redis caches answers to speed up repeated questions.

---

**Q: What questions can the app be used for?**  
A: Ask anything related to the content of your uploaded research papers.

---

**Q: How can I add new research papers?**  
A: Upload PDFs on the "Metadata Inventory" page and run the pipeline.

---

**Q: Do input papers need to be in PDF?**  
A: Yes, only PDF files are supported.

---

**Q: Does the app support conversation/multi-turn chat?**  
A: No, each question is answered independently (not a true conversation).

---

**Q: Can I use my own LLM?**  
A: Yes, you can swap in any local or open-source LLM if you wish.

---

**Q: Where is my data stored?**  
A: All data stays on your local machine.

---

**Q: Is my data private?**  
A: Yes, nothing is sent to the cloud unless you use Azure LLMs.
